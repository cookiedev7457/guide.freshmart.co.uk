Full Rank Hierarchy

Public Tiers

Valued Customer

Standard group member and shopper.

Noted Customer

Recognized former staff or contributors.

Awaiting Training

Newly joined members ready to begin staff training.

Lower Ranks (LR)

Junior Store College

Entry-level trainee for store operations.

Junior Security

Entry-level trainee for security operations.

Store College

Passed basic training; working on the floor.

Security College

Passed basic training; patrolling the store.

Senior Store College

Experienced retail staff member.

Senior Security

Experienced safety staff member.

Middle Ranks (MR)

Department Supervisor

Oversees daily LR operations and helps with trainings.

Assistant Store Manager

Management support and trainee evaluation.

Store Manager

Leads the MR team and hosts official sessions.

High Ranks (HR)

Executive Store Manager

Handles staff complaints and group efficiency.

Chief Operations Officer

Directly manages all MR/HR performance.

Employment Director

In charge of promotions and blacklist management.

Executive Leadership

Vice-Chairman

Second-in-command. Oversees all group departments.

Group Chairman

Founder and Owner of Fresh-Mart.
