Staff Rules

Strict Enforcement

Failure to follow these rules will result in immediate disciplinary action.

1. Professionalism

No unprofessional behavior or "trolling" while on duty. Proper grammar is expected.

2. Respect

Be helpful and stay respectful to all users at all times.

3. Admin Usage

Do not leak internal staff information and do not use admin commands for personal gain.
