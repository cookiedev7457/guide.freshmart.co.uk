Management Tier

Responsible for overseeing store operations and ensuring LRs are performing their duties correctly.

Supports the Store Manager in daily tasks. Authorized to assist in training sessions.

Requirement:

Promoted from Supervisor

Full oversight of the store. Can handle minor staff disputes and host official training sessions.

Requirement:

Management Excellence
