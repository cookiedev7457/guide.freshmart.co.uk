# Staff Promotions Guide

Welcome to the Fresh-Mart Low Rank team! This page explains how to get promoted.

### 📈 Promotion Requirements
* **Attendance:** You must attend at least 3 shifts per week.
* **Performance:** High efficiency at the registers and helpfulness to customers.
* **Professionalism:** Always follow the dress code and use grammar.

### 🚫 Points to Avoid
* Asking for promotions (will result in a delay).
* Disrespecting MR/HR members.
* Leaving your station without permission.
