Disciplinary Policy

High Ranks must follow the strict ladder of disciplinary action when handling staff misconduct.

Stage 1: Verbal Warning

Used for minor grammar slips, accidental unprofessionalism, or being AFK on duty.

Stage 2: Official Strike

Documented infraction. Given for repeated minor offenses or intentional rule breaking.

3 STRIKES = DEMOTION

Stage 3: Termination & Blacklist

Immediate removal from group for leaking, extreme trolling, or admin abuse.
