Public Ranks

Valued Customer

Standard group member and shopper at Fresh-Mart.

Noted Customer

Recognized individuals or former staff members.

Awaiting Training

New recruits ready to attend their first training session.

Lower Ranks (LR)

Junior Store/Security

Entry-level staff learning the basics of their department.

Store/Security College

Intermediate staff who have completed initial orientation.

Senior Store/Security

Experienced staff capable of working without constant supervision.

Department/Security Supervisor

Highest LR rank; helps guide new recruits on the shop floor.

High Ranks (HR)

Department Manager

Oversees specific store areas and manages LR schedules.

Assistant Store Manager

Supports the Store Manager in daily operations and discipline.

Team Leader

Authorized to host training sessions and manage store incidents.

Administration

Senior Team Leader

Senior officials who manage the HR team and store policy.

Executive Store Manager

Top-tier management responsible for all personnel and staff logs.

Executive Leadership

Board of Directors

Oversees group-wide operations and corporate strategy.

Chairman / Vice-Chairman

The final authority and owners of the Fresh-Mart brand.
