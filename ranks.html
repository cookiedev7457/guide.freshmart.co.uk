Rank Structure

A comprehensive guide to the roles and responsibilities within Fresh-Mart.

Public Tiers

Valued Customer

Supporters of the brand who enjoy shopping at our locations.

Requirement: Join the Group

Noted Customer

Former staff or influential community members recognized by leadership.

Requirement: Notable Contribution

Awaiting Training

The starting point for all aspiring staff. Eligible to attend trainings.

Requirement: Apply via Center

Staff Operations (LR)

Junior Store College

New retail staff learning register operations and stocking.

Requirement: Pass Training

Junior Security

Trainee safety officers learning patrol routes and conduct.

Requirement: Pass Training

Senior Security

Experienced safety officers trusted with handling larger incidents.

Requirement: High Activity

Department Supervisor

Ensures LRs are active and professional on the store floor.

Requirement: Leadership Merit

Management (HR)

Assistant Store Manager

Manages day-to-day staff issues and assists the Store Manager.

Requirement: HR Application/Invite

Store Manager

Complete oversight of the store. Manages training schedules.

Requirement: Management Excellence

Team Leader

Authorized to host official group training sessions.

Requirement: Hosting Certification

Ownership

Vice-Chairman

Oversees all department managers and high-level strategy.

Group Chairman

The ultimate authority and founder of Fresh-Mart.
