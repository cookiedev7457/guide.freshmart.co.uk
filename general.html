General HR Information

As a High Rank (HR) at Fresh-Mart, you represent the highest level of professionalism. You are responsible for the oversight of Middle Ranks (MR) and Lower Ranks (LR), ensuring the store operates smoothly and rules are enforced fairly.

Professionalism

HRs must maintain perfect grammar and a neutral tone at all times. Personal bias or "friendship favoritism" when promoting or punishing is strictly prohibited and results in a demotion.

Staff Management

You are expected to monitor the Training Center and the Store. If an MR is struggling with a session, it is your duty to step in and provide guidance without being condescending.

Confidentiality

Anything discussed in HR-only channels or meetings is strictly confidential. Leaking HR information to LRs or MRs is considered a betrayl of trust and will lead to a permanent blacklist.

Command Usage

Admin commands should only be used for moderation purposes (kick, ban, warn). Using "fun" commands (fly, speed, etc.) on yourself or others in public servers is forbidden.
