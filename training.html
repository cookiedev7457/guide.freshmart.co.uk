Strict Training Enforcement

Failure to comply with the script and/or the Host, may result in dismissal from the session.

Welcome to Fresh-Mart's Training Guide. Here you can find all needed information to host or train at any training. Please only Host/Co-Host trainings you have claimed unless stated otherwise by a Group Administrator+.

Host Responsibilities

  • Arrive at the training centre between XX:30 and XX:40
  • Ensure all trainers are assigned to a group.
  • Maintain a professional demeanor throughout the session.
  • Utilize the most updated version of the script found on this page.

Co-Host Responsibilities

  • Arrive at the training centre between XX:35 and XX:40
  • Post the correct format in the group shout at XX:45
  • Maintain a professional demeanor throughout the session.
  • Utilize the most updated version of the script if the Host is unavailable.
