Strict Enforcement

Failure to follow these rules will result in immediate termination.

Official Store Guidelines

1. Respect the Community

Be Kind:

Treat all shoppers and staff members with respect. Bullying, harassment, or "trolling" will result in a kick or ban.

Keep it Clean:

Follow the Roblox Terms of Service. This means no inappropriate language, bypasses, or suggestive outfit combinations.

2. Fair Shopping Etiquette

No Spamming:

Do not spam the chat or the "buy" buttons. It creates lag for everyone else.

Booth/Stall Rules:

If the game allows you to claim a stall, do not block other players' shops or use offensive text/images on your displays.

Line Up:

If there is a checkout or fitting room system, wait your turn patiently. No "glitching" through walls to cut the line.

3. Staff & Roleplay

Listen to Staff:

If a moderator or staff member gives you an instruction, please follow it.

Don't Ask for Robux:

Begging for currency or items is annoying to other players and often against the rules.

Roleplay Fairly:

If you are roleplaying as a security guard or shoplifter, keep it fun and consensual. Don't ruin the experience for people who just want to shop.

Admin & Enforcement

To keep the game safe, we use a "Three Strikes" system:

Warning

A verbal or UI-based reminder of the rules.

Kick

Removal from the current server.

Permanent Ban

For serious offenses (exploiting, hate speech, or repeated breaking of rules).
