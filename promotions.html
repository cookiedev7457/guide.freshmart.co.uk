How to get Promoted

Professionalism

Maintain a positive and professional attitude at all times while on duty.

Activity

Attend at least 2 training sessions weekly to stay sharp and eligible for rank-ups.

Clean Record

Follow all staff regulations meticulously without receiving any warnings.

Leadership

Demonstrate leadership and grammar skills while on-duty at the store.
