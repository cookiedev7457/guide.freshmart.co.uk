Promotion Policy

Learn how to level up within the Fresh-Mart team through activity and dedication.

How to get Promoted

  • Maintain a positive and professional attitude at all times.
  • Attend at least 2 training sessions per week.
  • Follow all staff regulations without receiving any warnings.
  • Demonstrate leadership skills while on-duty at the store.
